About Sunrise Academy

Two decades of excellence in education, shaping minds and building futures from Primary to 12th Standard.

Our Story

Founded in 2004, Sunrise Academy began with a simple yet powerful vision: to provide quality education that nurtures not just academic excellence but also character development and life skills. What started as a small institution with 50 students has grown into a thriving educational community of over 1,200 students.

Our journey has been marked by continuous innovation in teaching methodologies, infrastructure development, and a commitment to staying ahead of educational trends. We have consistently maintained our position as one of the region's leading educational institutions.

Today, we are proud to have graduated thousands of students who have gone on to excel in various fields, from medicine and engineering to arts and entrepreneurship, carrying with them the values and knowledge imparted at Sunrise Academy.

Our Mission

To provide a comprehensive, student-centered education that develops critical thinking, creativity, and character. We are committed to creating a nurturing environment where every student can discover their potential and develop the skills necessary for lifelong learning and success.

Our Vision

To be a leading educational institution that empowers students to become confident, compassionate, and capable global citizens. We envision a future where our graduates contribute meaningfully to society while maintaining strong ethical values and a commitment to excellence.
