About Sunrise Academy

Established with a vision to provide quality education, Sunrise Academy has been serving the community for over two decades. We offer comprehensive education from Primary to 12th Standard, focusing on academic excellence and character development.

Our mission is to create a nurturing environment where every student can discover their potential, develop critical thinking skills, and grow into responsible global citizens.
